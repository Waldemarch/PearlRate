How the columns work

  • Yield/day — base benchmark yield ÷ difficulty multiplier. Fixed by hardware, independent of token price.
  • Gross — yield × PRL price. Revenue before any costs.
  • Break-even rent/h — the max hourly rental a miner can pay and still profit (gross/h minus pool fee). Your pricing ceiling per card as a host.
  • Self-mine net/day — running your own card, paying only electricity.

Assumptions & caveats

  • Baseline yields from hashrate.no benchmarks (26 May 2026), Pearl Research Labs PRL — not the Solana "Perle" token.
  • Verified*  * yield measured on a real hashrate.no PRL benchmark (26 May 2026).
  • Estimated*  * no verified PRL benchmark — yield scaled from tensor-core throughput and shown (italic, in parentheses). Double-click any yield cell to override.
  • PRL trades on a thin market (SafeTrade) — paper valuations may not clear at size.
  • Network difficulty climbs fast (>9 EH/s already). Push the slider up to stress-test.
  • Not financial advice. DYOR.
